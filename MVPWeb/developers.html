WHAT IT MEANS TO BE A DEVELOPER

MVP Studio developers contribute to our community in two ways:

  1. They provide low–cost, custom software solutions to community–minded organizations and businesses AND
  2. They mentor an up and coming software development intern.

We're looking for reliable, enthusiastic self–starters who love working in a team environment. MVP Developers should have at least 3 years of experience working with custom code. We would prefer that you also have testing experience. Work hours and locations are flexible, but developers are expected to attend face–to–face meetings and be available for work sessions with fellow developers and interns.

If you are interested in developing with us, we would love for you to apply. However, if you have any questions, please feel free to contact us and we'll help you out.
