WHAT IT MEANS TO BE AN INTERN

MVP Studio interns learn by doing. Our interns work with one or more mentors from local firms to create custom tech solutions for community-oriented businesses, startups, and nonprofit organizations. We are looking for reliable, enthusiastic self-starters who love working in a team environment.

Internships are part-time and flexible-you'll get the experience of working as a consultant more than an employee when you intern at MVP Studio. The hours are flexible and, as an intern, you have the opportunity to use space at CodeChops, a local co-working studio, or from home. You are expected to attend team meetings, whether face-to-face or via internet; you also must be available for work sessions with mentors. Interns should also have some professional or academic coding experience.

If you are interested in interning with us, we would love for you to apply. However, if you have any questions, please feel free to contact us and we'll help you out.
