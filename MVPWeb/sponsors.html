WHAT IT MEANS TO BE A SPONSOR

MVP Studio sponsors play an incredible role of providing the means for everyone participating in MVP the chance for achieving the vision of creating better software and overall, better tech. You help us fulfill our mission of creating lasting connections to ensure that project ideas are heard and people could come together to make those projects happen.

MVP Studio is a nonprofit organization, so as a sponsor, you could get a tax write–off, and be apart of what true teamwork really is. You will get a quarterly report summary that will list fund allocations so you will know exactly how your money is being spent. Also, if you are interested in participating, you are more than welcome. Please contact us and we will help you with that.

If you are interested in sponsoring with us, we would love for you to apply. However, if you have any questions, please feel free to contact us and we'll help you out.
